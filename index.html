WE ARE OPEN EVERYDAY from
10AM - 7PM

Welcome! We are a unique, fun and funky clothing resale shop specializing in vintage. We have three locations all centrally located in the downtown areas of Hillcrest, Encinitas and Carlsbad. We are always buying and stock new inventory daily.


Stop In!

BUY

FRESH NEW FINDS DAILY! We are always buying and selling. On any given day we sort through approximately 1,000 pieces of clothing. We get everything from grandma's attic to movie stars' passé to 'worn once last season and onto the new.'

SELL

CASH FOR CLOTHES! We buy EVERYTHING... vintage, designer and new clothing. We are always on the look-out for unique and different, classic, high quality, nostalgic and simply just about everything. Let our experienced buyers take a look.

The Buy Counter CLOSES at 5pm daily.

TRADE

TRASH TO TREASURE! We guarantee you will not leave empty handed. We offer store credit for purchased clothing. Use this credit towards a trade purchase instead of receiving cash. Out with your old and in with the new.

Our Principles

Quality

We pride ourselves on quality items. We only buy garments in the best condition, free of stains, holes, wear and tear.

Responsibility

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, Reclaim, Repurpose. By buying and selling vintage items we contribute to an eco-friendly community.

Variety

We carry everything from costumes to wigs to new designer to trending items to brand names to vintage of all decades.
